# Passenger Manifest: Complete Index

## Overview

The maiden voyage of the Babel Elevator carries **500 passengers** aboard Climber One, departing Te Rarawa on August 4th, 2085. This manifest names every passenger, organized by deck assignment and access tier.

**Total Passengers:** 500
